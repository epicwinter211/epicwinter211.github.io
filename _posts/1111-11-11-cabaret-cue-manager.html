What is it?

The cabaret cue manager is a project that I worked on for a cabaret that I am participating in.

It is supposed to compile a few videos into one, and you can cue them into a fullscreen video.

How it works is, you upload a few videos, then you click 'compile video'. This should bring you into a fullscreen video.

In the fullscreen video, you can press space to cue the next video. To edit it or to go out of fullscreen, press escape.

To reset the video, press 'reset video'. This will delete all the videos. To add a video, press 'add video'.
